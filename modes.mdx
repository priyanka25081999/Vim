VIM 
Modes:
1. Normal mode: 
    * j - move the cursor downwards
    * k - move the cursor upwards
    * l - move in the right direction
    * h - move in the left direction
    * w - move the cursor forward one word at a time
    * b - move the cursor backward one word at a time
    * 8k - move the cursor 8times upwards on 8th line (use any n, here n=8)
    * 16j - move the cursor 16times downwards on 16th line (use any n, here n=16)
